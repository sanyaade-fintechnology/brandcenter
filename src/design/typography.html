Typography

SumUp's font-face for everything is called Museo Sans.

Created by the excellent exljbris Font Foundry in 2008 by Jos Buivenga we chose this young type face to represent SumUp throughout our communication. Apart from it's geometric but still humanistic appearance, Museo is a full featured OpenType font with support for many character sets, including i.e. Cyrillic.
